Overview

The interview you can't prepare a script for.

UnderFireAI is an AI-powered interview coaching platform built on a single insight: real interviews are unpredictable. Traditional interview prep tools give you a list of questions and let you rehearse answers. UnderFireAI gives you an AI interviewer with a hidden personality — a skeptic, a rapid-fire griller, a silent judge — and makes you adapt in real time. You don't know who you're sitting across from until the conversation starts.

The system borrows its core architecture from Kirra Companion's "blind date" backstory engine, adapted for professional interview contexts. Each AI interviewer has a unique personality profile with six behavioral traits — directness, depth preference, warmth, patience, technical focus, and skepticism — that drive how they respond to your answers, what follow-up questions they ask, and when they push harder or ease off.

Every response you give is analyzed in real time for STAR format compliance, clarity, confidence markers, technical depth, and relevance. At the end of a session, you get a detailed scorecard with category breakdowns, identified strengths, areas for improvement, and an AI-generated summary of how the interviewer perceived you — the feedback you'd never get from a real interview.

The Secret Sauce

Six hidden archetypes. You won't know which one you get.

Each interviewer archetype has distinct behavioral patterns, red flags that trigger skepticism, green flags that earn their respect, and mood shifts that evolve throughout the conversation. The user never sees the archetype label — they discover the personality through interaction, exactly like a real interview.

The Skeptic

"That's interesting, but can you give me a specific example?"

Doubts everything. Wants proof, metrics, and specifics. Vague answers trigger deeper probing. Impressed by concrete data, measurable outcomes, and candidates who bring receipts.

red flags: vague answers, no metrics · green flags: data, specifics, numbers

The Griller

"Let's go deeper on that architecture decision..."

Deep technical dives on every answer. Surface-level knowledge gets exposed fast. Loves when candidates admit what they don't know — honesty earns more respect than bluffing.

red flags: surface knowledge, bluffing · green flags: depth, intellectual honesty

The Friendly

"That's great! So tell me... what was the hardest part?"

Warm and supportive — until they aren't. Creates comfort, then surprises with hard questions when your guard is down. Arrogance and dismissiveness are instant red flags. Responds deeply to humility and authenticity.

red flags: arrogance, dismissiveness · green flags: humility, authenticity

The Silent Judge

"Mm-hm. Continue."

Minimal feedback. Short responses. Long pauses. Poker face. The most psychologically challenging archetype — designed to test candidates who need external validation. Internal scoring only revealed at the end.

red flags: need for validation · green flags: self-directed confidence

The Rapid Fire

"Good. Next question — you have 30 seconds."

Fast-paced, time pressure, quick follow-ups. Interruptions are part of the experience. Long-winded answers get cut short. Rewards concise, structured responses that get to the point immediately.

red flags: long-winded answers · green flags: concise, structured, fast

The Culture Fit

"Tell me about a time you disagreed with your team..."

Values-focused interviewer obsessed with team dynamics, collaboration, and conflict resolution. Blame-shifting and lone wolf mentality are immediate red flags. Loves collaboration stories and credit-sharing.

red flags: blame-shifting, solo credit · green flags: collaboration, empathy

Scoring Engine

The feedback loop real interviews never give you

UnderFireAI scores candidates at two levels: per-response analysis that evaluates each individual answer, and a session-level scorecard that synthesizes the entire interview into actionable feedback. The scoring engine identifies key moments — strong answers, weak answers, and turning points — giving candidates specific, timestamped data on where they won or lost the room.

Per-Response Analysis

STAR score (0–100) measures Situation-Task-Action-Result structure. Clarity score rates how structured and readable the answer was. Confidence score detects hedging language and uncertainty markers. Relevance score measures whether the question was actually answered. Depth score evaluates sufficient detail. Response time and word count tracked. Filler words flagged.

Session Scorecard

Overall score (0–100) as a weighted average across all categories. Five category breakdowns: communication, technical depth, behavioral examples, culture fit, and problem solving. Lists of strengths and improvements. AI-generated interviewer impression — how the hidden personality felt about you. Key moments identified with timestamps and impact ratings.

Interviewer Personality Traits

Each interviewer runs on six independent 0–100 trait scales. Directness: blunt vs. diplomatic. Depth Preference: surface vs. deep dives. Warmth: cold vs. friendly. Patience: rapid follow-ups vs. space to think. Technical Focus: soft skills vs. hard skills. Skepticism: trusting vs. needs proof.

Mood Evolution

Interviewer mood is not static — it shifts in real time based on the candidate's responses. A Skeptic who receives specific metrics may warm up. A Friendly interviewer who detects arrogance may turn cold. A Silent Judge reveals nothing until the final scorecard. Mood data is tracked per message and factored into the final session score.

Architecture

Kirra's engine, rebuilt for the boardroom

UnderFireAI is built on Next.js 15 with the App Router, TypeScript, and Supabase. The AI interview engine uses Anthropic's Claude and DeepSeek for conversation, OpenAI for text-to-speech, and Segmind for environment scene generation. Payments are handled through Stripe with full webhook lifecycle management. Approximately 37 files were adapted directly from the Kirra Companion codebase — auth, UI components, TTS, Stripe — with the interview-specific logic built from scratch.

Next.js 15 React 18 TypeScript 5.7 Supabase PostgreSQL Anthropic Claude DeepSeek OpenAI TTS Segmind Stripe Tailwind CSS Radix UI Framer Motion Zustand React Hook Form Zod Recharts pdf-parse

Frontend

Next.js 15 App Router with React 18. Component library built on Radix UI with Tailwind CSS styling. Framer Motion powers the animated landing page with floating elements, scroll-triggered counters, and parallax effects. Zustand for state management. Recharts for progress analytics. React Hook Form with Zod validation on all inputs.

Backend & Database

Supabase (PostgreSQL) with full Auth integration. 8 database tables: profiles, interviewers, interviewer_personality, user_resumes, interview_sessions, interview_messages, session_scores, and user_progress. Row Level Security on every table. Custom enums for interview_type, company_style, and session_status.

AI Pipeline

Anthropic Claude and DeepSeek power the conversational interview engine with cache_control for ephemeral context. System prompts inject interviewer personality, backstory, and mood state per message. OpenAI TTS generates spoken interviewer audio with selectable voices. Segmind generates photorealistic office environment scenes matched to company style.

API & Payments

10 API routes covering interview creation, session chat, pause/resume, session end, TTS generation, resume upload, and Stripe checkout/portal/webhook. Stripe integration handles Free/Pro/Premium subscription tiers with webhook verification for checkout completion, subscription updates, cancellations, and failed payments. Auth middleware protects all dashboard routes.

Security

Production-hardened, not prototype-patched

UnderFireAI follows the same security-first standard applied to every Allen Code Co application. Every table has RLS. Every route checks authentication. Every webhook verifies its signature. Resume uploads are validated and parsed server-side.

✓

Row Level Security

All 8 database tables enforce RLS policies. Users can only access their own sessions, scores, resume data, and progress. Interviewer personality data is read-only for authenticated users.

✓

Auth Middleware

Supabase Auth with session verification on every protected route. Dashboard, interview, and API routes enforce authentication before any data access or AI calls.

✓

Stripe Webhook Verification

All Stripe events verified using constructEvent() with the webhook signing secret. Subscription lifecycle events processed with proper status transitions across Free, Pro, and Premium tiers.

✓

Secure Resume Handling

Resume uploads validated for file type and size before server-side parsing with pdf-parse. Extracted data is structured and stored — raw files are not served to other users or exposed via public endpoints.

✓

AI Context Isolation

Anthropic API calls use cache_control with ephemeral type for conversation context. No user interview data persists in AI model memory between sessions. Each session is a clean context window.

✓

Input Validation

All user inputs validated with Zod schemas via React Hook Form. API routes sanitize and type-check every parameter. Interview configuration validated before session creation.
